Reading California teaches California Content Standards for pre-kindergarten throught 3rd grade. It is a concise and direct method for teaching reading. The advanced sound dictionary and practice is unique. It features consistent grouping of advanced sounds. Virtually any age child or adult needing basic reading skills can benefit from Reading California.

Reading California is created by a Reading Specialists with over 30 years experience in California Schools. Reading California bypasses the glitter and gets to the essentials of reading. Success with the five lessons depends on practicing reading with the sound turned off.

This program demonstrates mastery of the majority of California Kindergarten through 3rd grade content skills. These skills provide the basis for a life time of reading ease and success.

Reading essentials include: 1) 26 Sounds 2) 3 Letter Blending with short vowels 3) long vowels including magic e 4) practice with irregular words grouped together.

Enjoy and all the best from Reading California.
